Stage 0

Product Ideation

Here, the product is a hypothetical model. UX teams help brainstorm, standardize, rank, and choose the most promising model to test.

Stage 1

Problem Validation

Here, the product is a low-fidelity prototype. UX teams help validate problems, customer segments, and existing alternative solutions.

Stage 2

Solution Validation

Here, the product is a high-fidelity prototype or early MVP. UX teams help validate solutions, early adopters, and paths to revenue.

Stage 3

Channel Validation

Here, the product is a fully-launched MVP. UX teams help validate outbound channels, the MVP's Unique Value Proposition, and grow revenue.

Stage 4

Scale

Here, the product is evolving beyond the MVP. UX teams help validate inbound channels, cost structures, key metrics, and the product's Unfair Advantage.
